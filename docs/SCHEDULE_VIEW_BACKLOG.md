# Schedule View — Deferred Enhancements

Backlog of high-value future enhancements intentionally deferred from the initial Schedule view implementation.

## Scheduling & Planning

- **True goal scheduling** — Allow users to assign goals to specific dates or time blocks.
- **Weekly pacing recommendations** — Suggest how much work to do per week to hit a deadline.
- **Goal dependency chains** — Show which goals depend on others completing first on the timeline.
- **Drag to adjust deadline** — Allow deadline adjustment by dragging on the calendar.

## Forecasting & Simulation

- **Scenario simulation** — "If you increase pace by 20%, you'd finish by…" interactive projections.
- **Seasonal trend detection** — Detect weekday/weekend or seasonal patterns in pace for smarter forecasts.
- **Confidence intervals** — Show forecast uncertainty range instead of a single projected date.
- **Regression-based forecasting** — Replace simple linear pace with weighted recent trend analysis.

## Milestones

- **Custom milestone definitions** — Let users define their own milestone percentages or absolute values.
- **Milestone notifications** — Alert when a milestone is reached or approaching.
- **Milestone celebration** — Trigger celebratory UI when a milestone is hit.

## Cross-feature Integration

- **Today view risk alerts** — Surface "at risk" goals directly in the Today/Day view.
- **Dashboard forecast widget** — Show upcoming deadlines and forecasts on the Dashboard.
- **Routine-goal linking** — Show which routines contribute to which goals on the schedule.

## Views & Export

- **Timeline / Gantt view variant** — Linear timeline instead of month grid calendar.
- **Calendar export / iCal sync** — Export goal deadlines and milestones to external calendars.
- **Multi-month overview** — Zoom out to see 3-6 months of goal events at once.
- **Year view** — Annual overview showing goal distribution and completion patterns.

## Analytics

- **Pace trend charts** — Show how pace has changed over time within the Schedule view.
- **Category convergence analysis** — Highlight when goals in the same category cluster.
- **Completion velocity tracking** — Track how quickly goals are being completed over time.
